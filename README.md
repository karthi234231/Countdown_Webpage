# 🎉 New Year Countdown

A sleek, animated countdown that automatically ticks down to every upcoming New Year — no manual updates needed, ever.

**🔗 Live → [neewyearr.netlify.app](https://neewyearr.netlify.app)**

---

### Preview

![Countdown Preview](blackFish.jpg)

---

### Features

- **Auto-updating** — switches to the next year's countdown automatically
- **Flip card animation** — smooth scoreboard-style digit transitions
- **Jan 1 celebration** — shows a Happy New Year message for 24 hours, then resets
- **Single file** — entire app lives in one `index.html`, no build step
- **Responsive** — works on mobile, tablet, and desktop

---

### Tech

`HTML` `CSS` `Vanilla JavaScript`

---

### How it works

| Date | Behaviour |
|---|---|
| Any day except Jan 1 | Counts down to next Jan 1 |
| Jan 1 (within 24 hrs) | 🎉 Celebrates the New Year |
| After 24 hrs on Jan 1 | Auto-switches to next year |
